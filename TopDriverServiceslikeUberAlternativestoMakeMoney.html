What is a ride share app for drivers?

Being a ride sharing driver is similar to a taxi driver but with more freedom. You can set your own hours. Work whenever you want and as much as you want. The more you work the more money you make, however, you can also just work a few hours a week to make some extra money or to cover the costs of driving to work each day.

There are no minimum hours you have to work for Lyft, Uber, or other services.

“The good thing is you're your own boss. If you need to take time off or adjust your schedule no one is going to say NO.” Lyft driver John Gardiner says.

How ridesharing works:

Most ridesharing apps like Uber or Lyft match passengers to the closest drivers based on their GPS location. You’ll get a notification asking you to accept or decline a ride. Once you accept it, you’ll need to drive to pick up the passenger and then drop him off at the end point.

Lyft ride sharing app pays drivers per minute and per mile.

DoorDash Driving Delivery Jobs

 

DoorDash is a ‘dinner-on-demand’ service. People can order meals from restaurants in their city and DoorDash sends their delivery guys to deliver the food to wherever the clients are in town.

With DoorDash you get paid when you deliver an order. The client might be at home or he might be in the park. You can deliver by car, motorbike, scooter, or bicycle.

Unlike ride-sharing services, with DoorDash you don’t have to worry about annoying passengers or passengers dirtying your car since no one sits in it apart from you.

How does DoorDash work?

Using the DoorDash app you get notifications asking if to accept or decline a job near you. Once you accept it, you’ll need to pick up the food from the restaurant and then deliver it to the client. You’ll get paid once you’ve delivered the food to the client.

DoorDash driver requirements:

·         You need to be 18+ and have a smartphone (so you can use their app).

·         Your own vehicle (motorbike, bicycle or car).

·         If you’re delivering with a car, you’ll need to have a valid driver’s license, insurance, and a clean driving record.

·         Drivers cannot have any major violations in the last 7 years, including: DUI, Reckless Driving, Homicide or Assault.

·         Not more than 3 incidents in the past 3 years.

 

DoorDash runs a background check on all applicants. It can take a few days to 2 weeks to get approved to become a DoorDasher.

The nice thing is you can work for DoorDash even if you don’t have a car!

DoorDash locations:

DoorDash currently operates in Atlanta, Boston, Charlotte, Chicago, Columbus, Dallas / Ft. Worth, Denver, Houston, Indianapolis, Los Angeles, Minneapolis, Manhattan, Brooklyn, Orange County, Phoenix, San Diego, San Francisco, San Jose / Silicon Valley, SF East Bay, Seattle, Toronto, Vancouver, Washington, D.C, San Antonio, and Nashville.

SideCar Ridesharing Car Service

 

Sidecar is a small player in the ride sharing industry.

They have very few passengers and therefore their drivers don’t make much money.

Unlike Lyft SideCar doesn’t offer any bonuses including signup bonuses, earnings bonuses, prime time bonuses, peak hour bonuses, weekly challenges with bonuses, hourly bonuses, weekly pay guarantees or other bonuses.

They’re trying to grow but aren’t having much success.

Nevertheless, they’re a car service to keep an eye on. Maybe one day they’ll grow.

Sidecar’s pricing structure is different than the other ride sharing apps. With SideCar, users set their own pricing, and offer up-front pricing for a ride. There are now higher rates during Prime Time, which makes it nice for passengers but not so attractive for drivers.
